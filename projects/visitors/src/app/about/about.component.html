About us

What we do

Promoting education for national development

The Association of Private School Owners of Nigeria (APSON) is the official umbrella body of all proprietors of private schools and other Non Governmetal Organizations (NGOs) engaged in education related matters in Nigeria.

Founded in 1995, the association was created to promote the interest of private school owners and improve the standard of quality eduction in Nigeria. The association is duly registered with corporate Affairs commission with certificate number 082240.

As the official flagship organization of private school owners in Nigeria, we are constantly building an organization that puts it's members welfare at the core center of our decision making process.

APSON provides a strong support system that ensures that members are protected from undue harrassments and can operate smoothly without encumbrances and hindrance.

We constantly collaborate with the relevant governmental agencies and other stakeholders in education sector.
